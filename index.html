The Calculation Logic

How we determine your max score potential:

20,000 + (obstacles - 16) * 2,500

Base: Reaching 5x multiplier gives 20,000 points.[cite: 2]

Scaling: (x-16) is the count of obstacles passed while at 5x.[cite: 2]

Value: Each of those obstacles is worth 2,500 points.[cite: 2]
